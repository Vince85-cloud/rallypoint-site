About RallyPoint

RallyPoint Fitness is designed for people who want more than a simple workout log. The platform combines workout tracking, outdoor sessions, routines, military readiness, performance planning, and connected-device integrations into one training system.

RallyPoint supports general fitness users, hybrid athletes, endurance athletes, and tactical or military-focused trainees who need structure, visibility, and progression.

Device And Platform Integrations

RallyPoint is being built to connect with major wearable and fitness ecosystems so users can centralize training and recovery data in one place.

  • Apple Health / HealthKit
  • Google Health Connect
  • Garmin Connect
  • Strava
  • Fitbit
  • WHOOP
  • Oura
