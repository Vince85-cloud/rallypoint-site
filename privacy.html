RallyPoint Fitness Privacy Policy

Effective date: March 15, 2026

RallyPoint Fitness ("RallyPoint", "we", "our", or "us") provides training, workout logging, performance tracking, recovery, and wearable integration features. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and what choices users have.

1. Information We Collect

  • Account information, such as email address and login credentials.
  • Workout and training data, including sessions, exercises, routines, and performance logs.
  • Health and wearable data, when users choose to connect supported health or wearable platforms.
  • Community content, including posts, comments, and shared training updates.
  • Technical and device information needed to operate and secure the app.

2. Wearable And Health Data

If a user connects Apple Health, Health Connect, Garmin, Strava, Fitbit, WHOOP, Oura, or other supported platforms, RallyPoint may access permitted data such as workouts, heart rate, sleep, steps, recovery, readiness, and similar training-related metrics.

RallyPoint only accesses connected data when the user authorizes the integration and grants the relevant permissions.

3. How We Use Information

  • Provide workout logging, programs, calculators, analytics, and coaching features.
  • Sync training and recovery data from connected wearable and health platforms.
  • Help users monitor performance, progression, and readiness.
  • Support community features such as posts, comments, and engagement.
  • Maintain security, troubleshoot issues, and improve the app.

4. Data Sharing

RallyPoint does not sell users' personal information. We may share data with service providers or integration partners only as needed to operate the app, complete authorized integrations, support analytics, or comply with legal obligations.

5. User Choices

  • Users can choose whether to connect wearable and health platforms.
  • Users can disconnect supported integrations where available.
  • Users can request account-related support or deletion inquiries by contacting us.

6. Data Security

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational measures to protect the information we manage. No system can guarantee absolute security.

7. Children’s Privacy

RallyPoint Fitness is not intended for children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

8. Contact

For privacy questions or requests, contact:

rallypointfitnessapp@gmail.com

9. Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the effective date on this page.
