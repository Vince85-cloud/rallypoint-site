RallyPoint Fitness Privacy Policy

Effective date: March 23, 2026

RallyPoint Fitness ("RallyPoint", "we", "our", or "us") provides training planning, workout logging, session execution, coaching, recovery support, community features, and connected wearable integrations. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and what choices users have.

1. Information We Collect

  • Account details such as email address, sign-in credentials, and profile information.
  • Training data including workouts, sessions, programs, performance logs, and planning history.
  • Recovery, readiness, and wearable-linked information when a user authorizes a supported connection.
  • Community content such as posts, comments, messages, and coaching inquiry details.
  • Technical and device information used to secure, operate, and improve the service.

2. Wearable And Health Data

If a user chooses to connect Apple Health, Health Connect, Garmin, Strava, Fitbit, WHOOP, Oura, or another supported platform, RallyPoint may receive permitted data such as workouts, activity, sleep, heart rate, recovery, readiness, steps, or similar metrics.

RallyPoint only accesses connected data after user authorization and only within the permissions granted by the user and the integration provider.

3. How We Use Information

  • To provide workout logging, program delivery, coaching, analytics, readiness, and specialty training features.
  • To sync and present authorized training and recovery data from connected services.
  • To improve planning, execution, retention, and product personalization features.
  • To support community participation, coaching inquiries, and premium experiences.
  • To maintain security, troubleshoot problems, and operate the service responsibly.

4. Data Sharing

RallyPoint does not sell personal information. We may share data with service providers, infrastructure vendors, and integration partners when needed to operate the app, support authorized connections, fulfill user-requested functionality, or comply with legal obligations.

5. User Choices

  • Users can decide whether to connect supported wearable and health platforms.
  • Users can disconnect integrations where the product exposes that control.
  • Users can contact RallyPoint for account, support, or deletion-related requests.

6. Data Security

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational safeguards to protect the information we handle. No system can guarantee absolute security.

7. Children's Privacy

RallyPoint Fitness is not intended for children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

8. Contact

For privacy questions, support requests, or data inquiries, contact:

9. Updates To This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the effective date on this page.
